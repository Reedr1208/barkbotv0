ChattyHound ("we", "us") helps people discover adoptable shelter dogs and chat
with an AI companion about each dog. This policy explains what we collect, how we
use it, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

- **Email address** — only if you choose to sign in. We use it as your account
  identifier to save your data and sync it across devices. Signing in is
  optional; you can browse and chat as a guest.
- **Match preferences** — the gender, age, size, and location filters you set.
- **Saved dogs** — the dogs you "heart."
- **Chat messages** — the messages you send to a dog and the AI's replies, so we
  can show your conversation history.
- **Limited technical data** — when you chat, our servers may record your IP
  address and approximate city (derived from your network connection) for
  security, abuse prevention, and analytics. Standard device and diagnostic data
  may also be provided by the app stores.

We do **not** collect precise GPS location, contacts, photos, or payment
information, and the app does not include third-party advertising or tracking
SDKs.

## How we use your information

- To provide the core experience: showing dogs, saving favorites, and powering
  AI chat.
- To save your preferences, favorites, and chat history to your account.
- To improve matching quality and the overall service.
- To keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

## AI processing

Messages you send in chat are processed by our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate
responses about the dog. Please don't share sensitive personal information in
chat. AI responses are informational and may be inaccurate — always confirm
medical, behavioral, and adoption details with the shelter.

## Service providers

We share data only with vendors that help us run the app, under their respective
terms:

- **Supabase** — database and image storage.
- **OpenAI** — generating AI chat responses.
- **Vercel** — hosting our API.

Dog information is sourced from publicly available shelter listings. We do not
sell your personal information.

## Data retention and deletion

You can permanently delete all of your data at any time from within the app:
**Profile → Delete my account & data**. This removes your preferences, saved
dogs, and chat history from our servers. You can also email us at
{CONTACT_EMAIL} to request deletion. Guests can clear data stored on their
device from the same screen.

## Children's privacy

ChattyHound is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK), and
we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a
child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR in the EEA/UK, CCPA in California), you may
have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data. To
exercise these rights, use in-app deletion or contact us at {CONTACT_EMAIL}.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No
method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute
security.
