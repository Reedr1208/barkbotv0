```

```

**Promotional Text** (≤170 chars, editable anytime without review)
```
New rescue pups added every day. Chat with a dog, learn their personality, and find the one who fits your life. 🐾
```

**Keywords** (≤100 chars, comma-separated, no spaces needed)
```
dog,adopt,adoption,rescue,shelter,puppy,pet,pets,animal,chat,ai,dogs,humane,foster,find a dog
```

**Description** (≤4000 chars)
```
Meet adoptable shelter dogs in a whole new way.

Instead of scrolling past the same generic profiles, ChattyHound lets you chat with each dog to get a feel for their personality, quirks, energy level, and the kind of home they're hoping to find. Ask what makes them happy, whether they're good with kids, or what to know before you meet — and discover the pup who truly fits your life.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Chat with a dog — a friendly AI companion brings each dog's profile to life.
• Smart matching — set your preferences (size, age, energy, location) and we prioritize dogs who fit.
• Save your favorites — heart the dogs you love and pick up right where you left off.
• Real shelter listings — browse adoptable dogs sourced from local shelters.
• Easy sharing — found a dog a friend would love? Send them straight to the pup's page.

SHELTER INFO COMES FIRST
ChattyHound is a playful companion to help you discover dogs — not a replacement for the shelter. The official shelter page is always the authority for medical history, behavior, and adoption eligibility. We show when each listing was last checked and link you straight to the shelter.

Start sniffing and find your new best friend. 🐶

Adoptable dog data is sourced from publicly available shelter listings and changes frequently — always confirm availability with the shelter.
```
